Current Projects

ODC

Offline Data Collector

As a part of the Smart India Hackathon 2020, Hexify is developing a system for offline data collection for schools under the Government of Sikkim . The internet connection in Sikkim is not very stable. Hence, it is difficult for schools to submit data to the govt. officials in different levels - block, district, state. ODC will be an offline first data collection system that first stores the collected data offline and then syncs with the govt. servers whenever a stable internet connection is available.

GenC

Private Bus Concession Card Generator

Educational institutions issue concession cards to their students for availing discounts on bus tickets on privately owned public transport buses. GenC is a software that aims to make the process of issuing the concession cards in the proper format specified by the Motor Vehicles Department, Kerala, much easier.
